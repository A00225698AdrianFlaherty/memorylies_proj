Welcome to Memory Lies | Bréag Cuimhne
a work-in-progress piece that explores, memory, time, and technology.

It allowed the maker some space to explore different avenues of making new experiences using digital tools. This including binaural recording and mixing approaches various digital formats as a device for theatre-making, and to research the wider ideas around symbolic interaction and how this could affect our lives.

The piece was created in a national stage of emergency that continues today. An emergency that could continue long into the future.
